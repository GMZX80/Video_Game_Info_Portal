## Decimal Date Policy

The incoming timeline uses decimal-style event labels such as `1983.4`. Public copy should not use those labels because they are ambiguous. Replace them with readable date ranges:

- `.1` to `.3`: early year
- `.4` to `.6`: mid year
- `.7` to `.9`: late year
- If source evidence gives a month or exact date, use that instead.
- If no corroborating source exists, use `c.` plus the year or `date not yet verified`.

SVG coordinate values in the HTML also resemble dates. Values such as `1930.0`, `2040.0` and similar coordinate/geometry numbers are not historical claims and should be ignored by date audits.
