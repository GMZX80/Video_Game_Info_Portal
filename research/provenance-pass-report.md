## 3. Evidence Supporting Identification

- Kevin Blake's "Tynesoft Boys Club Part One" is the key first-person retrospective source for the two staff photographs and captioned names.
- Private June 2026 Phil Scott testimony is recorded only as a corroboration lead; no private message text is stored in the repository.
- MobyGames supports several authority-name and alias normalisations, especially David Lloyd / Dave Croft, Michael Landreth, Jools Jameson, Bruce Nesbit, David Mann and Michael Hedley.
- Companies House confirms Eutechnyx Limited was incorporated as Zeppelin Games Limited on 30 September 1987 and later changed name through Merit Studios (Europe) Limited to Eutechnyx Limited.
- Ubisoft's current Newcastle page supports a 40-plus-year Newcastle presence for Ubisoft Reflections.

## 8. Proposed Corrections

- Public photo captions should render from `assets/data/photos.json` and say "caption attributed to source; further corroboration pending."
- Replace all decimal timeline labels with readable approximations or sourced dates.
- Use David Lloyd as the authority name, retaining Dave Croft / David Croft as aliases.
- Use Michael Landreth as the authority name, retaining Mike Landruff / Mikbox as caption variants.
- Upgrade Zeppelin incorporation date to 30 September 1987 where legal-company wording is used.
- Treat title credits as credits, not employment.
- Render only legal continuity as solid lines; staff/founder/skills heritage stays dashed; publishing and commissioning remain visually separate.
