What qualifies for the North East Collection?

A keyword match is only a lead.

Confirmed entries require a source-backed connection such as an organisation based in the region, a North East office/team, a North East-developed version, or an external publisher linked to North East-authored work. Publisher/index metadata is useful evidence of appearance in the magazines, but it does not by itself prove developer, employer or location claims.

Publication status

Strongest at indexes, cautious on people.

The current collection is strongest at magazine and organisation indexing. Named developer career histories, employment claims and photograph identifications remain under active verification, so unsupported biographies are not being created to inflate the person count.
