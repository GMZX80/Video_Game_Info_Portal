Super Gran is included as a Tier 3 exemplar because the current public magazine datastore can identify an archive-index record, but not yet a complete game history.

The Globalnet SPOT record supports a title and a Tynesoft publisher/label appearance. It does not by itself verify development, staff roles, platform-specific authorship or a complete release chronology.

That is the point of this page. Index-only records can be public and useful, but they must not look as complete as a researched story.
